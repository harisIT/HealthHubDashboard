Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update patient information?

Navigate to the "Patient Records" page. Locate the patient you wish to update in the table. Click on the "Edit" button next to their name. A modal will appear where you can modify their details. Click "Save Changes" to apply your updates.

Can I schedule appointments for multiple doctors?

Yes, on the "Appointments" page, click "Schedule New Appointment". In the form, you can select the desired doctor from a dropdown or type their name if they are new. The system supports scheduling for any doctor you add.

Where can I view hospital performance analytics?

The "Analytics" page provides various charts and metrics, including patient demographics, departmental patient volume, patient status distribution, and appointments over time. This gives you a comprehensive overview of hospital performance.

How do I change my profile password?

Go to the "Settings" page. In the "User Profile" section, you will find fields to enter a new password and confirm it. Enter your desired new password and click "Save Profile Changes."
